Your generous donations allow our team to achieve great things!

Please consider donating any amount you can to support our Student Launch team. Funds received will go toward helping the team purchase structural and electronic components, rocket motors, consumable supplies, software, hardware, and any of the myriad other things that go into building a large rocket and experimental payload. In addition, donations allow the team to travel to Launch Week competition in Huntsville, AL at the Marshall Space Flight Center, where teams from around the country will fly their rockets and conduct their experiments.

Donations are collected through PayPal and deposited in the team's account at Piedmont Virginia Community College. The donation site was set up by David Oxford, whose name you may see during the donation process, along with fundraising@piedmontlaunch.org. Credit card donations will appear as PAYPAL *FUNDRAISING.

Thank you for your kind support!
