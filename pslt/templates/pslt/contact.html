The Piedmont Student Launch Team is looking for ambitious people to participate in the 2018-2019 NASA Student Launch Initiative. If you have ever flown a model rocket, watched a rocket launch on TV, dreamed of mankind's future among the stars, or enjoy coordinating complex projects for a great cause, the Piedmont Student Launch Team may be the right place for you. On this team your unique and innovative skills will get put to use solving challenging problems with like-minded teammates and some of the brightest minds at NASA.

For more information, or to join in the fun, send a message to info@piedmontlaunch.org

For more information on our outreach activities, email our Director of Engagement & Outreach: outreach@piedmontlaunch.org
